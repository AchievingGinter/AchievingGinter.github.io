## Stocks

Stocks are interesting, for a variety of reasons. They represent an actual company, or some other entity and help reflect how the public percieves the management and essential value of the company. I started my first round of investing, with little money, to experiment with the market. 

## Common-Sense Investing and Picks
I decided to predict market growths in key economic environments for the most part and use this as a basis of my investments. Generally growth in one market will coincide with another, and stability in surrounding markets that it may depend on will help regulate its volatiy. 

### My stocks are:

IMGN

I choose this because of some research on cell-receptors. Since they deal largely in cancer research, namely antibody-drug conjugates, they should be able to produce reliable results. Constants affecting the company are population, general health, and obviously cancer. General health is quite bad and so cancer is more likely to occur. Given population is increasing, along with poor general health in our populations, they were a solid choice at the time. Up 250~

OPTT

A company that uses movement of the ocean to produce electricity. I think renewable energy is important and the more we invest in it, the higher priority it will become to producers of said energy. Further, electricity is expensive when you're offshore. After investigating the buoys and how they worked, I'm up around 500%. I short-sold 50% of my shares during high volatity peak session to reduce risk. 

LLNW

I didn't research much about this company. They are up 12% yet have gone down 31% in the last month. 

BIOC 

Up 50%. Same thing as IMGN, basically helps detect cells for cancer. 

XLK

Up 12%. An Index of the S&P 500. I didn't do much research on this one either. 

AMC

AHT
